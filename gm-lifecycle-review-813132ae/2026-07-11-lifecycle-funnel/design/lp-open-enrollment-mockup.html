Before you book

Questions about open enrollment.

What exactly is waived?

The one-time $65 waitlist fee that normally holds your family's place in line. It is waived when you book your tour through this page and complete that tour. The fee is not charged upfront while your tour is pending.

What happens if I have to cancel or miss my tour?

Your application does not activate, and the standard $65 waitlist fee applies. Reschedule instead of cancelling; the waiver stays available for any tour time you complete.

Can I join the waitlist without touring?

You're welcome to apply without a tour at any time on our standard application, but the fee waiver on this page applies only when a tour is booked and completed.

Does the tour itself cost anything?

No. Tours are always part of getting to know us at Growing Mindfully. This page waives the separate $65 waitlist fee, not the tour.

What ages can tour?

Every age we serve, six weeks through preschool. You'll see the room that fits your child's age on your visit.

Where are you located?

750 N Street, top floor, in downtown Sacramento, a five minute walk from the Capitol. Hours are Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
