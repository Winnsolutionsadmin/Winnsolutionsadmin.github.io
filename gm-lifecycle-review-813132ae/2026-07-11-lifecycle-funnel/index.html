T2Exit winback popupClicks T1 but tries to leave the reserve page. Offers a tour, routes to /tour/, tracks the booking.
T3Book a tour, waive the enrollment fee+24h, still no tour. Different picture, tracked CTA to /tour/.
T4Post-tour promo1h after the tour ends, only if Ashley marked it attended. Reserve same day, the $45 enrollment fee is waived.
Counsel guardrails baked in: T4 never fires on an unmarked tour (suppress plus auto-ping Ashley). The $45 waiver deadline is end of the tour's calendar day, not 5pm, so afternoon tours are not a 30-minute dark pattern. One $45 waiver max per family (first-writer-wins).

5 · Counsel panel verdict

Five models, two adversarial rounds. What they endorsed, and where they reversed my draft recommendations.

Endorsed

Wave sequencing (+86 to +95), V3 hold (+75 to +96), end-of-day $45 deadline not 5pm, grid as row attribute, and unanimously the cohort-predicate bug fix (+82 to +100): the literal fee_paid=1 trigger silently skips the fee-waived Open Enrollment cohort, the very cohort under test.

Reversed my draft

T1 sends promptly, not +24h (peak intent). Drop the lifetime 3-email cap (childcare cycles run months). Never send T4 on an unmarked tour (unanimous). All three are folded into the plan.

Panel's top flagged risk: launching on the existing clickwrap, where a waived family signs terms describing a non-refundable fee they never paid (-44 to -80 across all five). Your D4 ruling stands; the panel notes the fix is a single conditional sentence, surfaced below as decision 1.
